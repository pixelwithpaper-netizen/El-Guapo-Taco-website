Who We Are

Our Story

🌮
"Good food doesn't need a fancy address."

El Guapo Taco is a family-run taco truck born from a deep love of bold, authentic flavors and the belief that the best food is made with heart — and served straight to the streets. Based out of Canutillo, TX, we proudly serve the communities of Las Cruces, NM and El Paso, TX.

Every taco, every tray, every plate we put out carries the spirit of family tradition. Whether you spot our blue truck at one of our regular spots or book us for your next big event, you're getting the real deal — made fresh, made bold, made with love.
